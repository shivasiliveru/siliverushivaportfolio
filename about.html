I build things that solve real problems.

Hi, I'm Shiva — a Computer Science undergraduate at JNTU, passionate about the craft of building software that goes beyond the screen. I believe the best code isn't just functional — it's meaningful.

My journey started with curiosity: tinkering with websites, breaking things apart to understand how they worked, and slowly putting together projects that could actually help people. That curiosity hasn't stopped.

"The most exciting part of building software isn't the code itself — it's the moment someone uses what you built and it just works."

Today I work across the full stack — from crafting responsive frontends to building Python-based backends and integrating AI capabilities. My recent focus has been on AI-powered applications: platforms that learn, adapt, and give users genuine value, not just features.

I've built Kareer Guide, an AI career platform for students; Kisan Sathi, a farming assistant designed specifically for rural communities in India; and Emotion Detector, a real-time computer vision project — each one teaching me something new about technology and people.

I'm currently open to internships and would love to work with a team where I can learn fast, contribute meaningfully, and help build things that matter.
